          	<h1><strong>Introdução ao POPStarter</strong></h1>

	
	<p>POPStarter is a launcher which lets you play your PS1 games in combination with $ony&#8217;s PS1 emulator for PS2 (known as &#8220;POPS&#8221; or &#8220;SLBB-00001&#8221;). Unlike the previous POPStarter versions and the proofs of concept, POPStarter r13 does <span class="caps">NOT</span> contain the emulator itself or libraries that belong to $ony. It is safe to publish in forums/sites that don&#8217;t tolerate warez stuff, as long as it&#8217;s not repacked with the decrypted emulator files or things like a PS <span class="caps">BIOS</span>.</p>

	<p>POPStarter is available for free download. If you spent an outrageous amount of money in ordering a <span class="caps">HDD</span> that contains preinstalled games and POPStarter, <span class="caps">YOU</span> <span class="caps">HAVE</span> <span class="caps">BEEN</span> <span class="caps">ROBBED</span>. Never purchase a physical copy of POPStarter when the &#8220;seller&#8221; wants more $$$ than the media price and the shipping fees.</p>

	<h2><em>Features:</em> </h2>

	<ul>
		<li>Suporte ao <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PSX_%28video_game_console%29"><span style="font-weight: bold; ">PSX DVR</span></a></li>
		<li>Suporte ao <span class="caps">CD, </span>, SMB, <span class="caps">HDD</span> &#38; <span class="caps">USB</span></li>
		<li>Suporta Trilhas<span class="caps">CDDA</span></li>
		<li>Suporta <span class="caps">VMC</span></li>
		<li>Suporte ao In-Game-Reset</li>
		<li>Atalhos In-Game</li>
		<li>Moto de Trapaça Embutido</li>
		<li>Patch automáticos para jogos <span class="caps">PAL</span> e <span class="caps">NTSC</span></li>
		<li>480p support (<span class="caps">NOT</span> reliable <span class="caps">ATM</span>)</li>
		<li><span class="caps">GTE</span> widescreen hack</li>
		<li>Suporte a Multi-discos</li>
		<li>Suporte a analógicos e vibrações</li>
	</ul>
